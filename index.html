Biography

Sarah J Ortiz is an up and coming artist born and raised in Brooklyn, NY. Sarah is multi-talented and is currently versed in many talents such as acting, improv comedy, singing, modeling, dancing and tv host. Sarah can speak in many accents and dialects such as British, New York, Spanish and Southern. She has training from Monologue Method, The Actors Project NYC, TBG Arts Center for Film & TV, and Mark Stolzenberg Actors Studio.

Recently Sarah J debuted as host for WILLiFEST, and her own TV show called Sarah J UNCUT (available now on the WILLiFEST channel on FOOTPRINT Network). Sarah J has recently filmed several productions. She has been a guest on the Donna Drake Show as well as The Mel Robbins Show. Sarah J began her film career as a role-playing P.a.a Garcia playing a rookie cop in Keys and Cuffs, and was also requested to be in Angie Films Group’s other recent productions including Chill Pill, Block Party, and Run. She is also in the film Hypothetically as a jogger. These films are set to release in Spring and Summer 2020.
